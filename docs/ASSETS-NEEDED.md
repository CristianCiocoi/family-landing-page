## ðŸ“ Photo Guidelines

### General Tips:

- **Lighting**: Natural daylight or well-lit spaces
- **Focus**: Sharp, clear images (no blur)
- **Composition**: Clean, professional framing
- **Quality**: High resolution before optimization
- **Authenticity**: Real photos of your actual work

### What to Avoid:

- âŒ Blurry or out-of-focus images
- âŒ Poor lighting (too dark or overexposed)
- âŒ Cluttered backgrounds
- âŒ Watermarked stock photos
- âŒ Images from other businesses (copyright issues)

---

## ðŸ› ï¸ Image Optimization

Before adding images to your site:

1. **Resize** to recommended dimensions
2. **Compress** using tools like:
   - [TinyPNG](https://tinypng.com/)
   - [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app/)
   - [ImageOptim](https://imageoptim.com/) (Mac)
3. **Target file sizes**:
   - Hero image: < 500KB
   - Portfolio images: < 300KB each
   - Logo: < 50KB
   - Other images: < 200KB each
